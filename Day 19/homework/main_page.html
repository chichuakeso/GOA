ALIEN STAGE




what is alien stage?

Alien Stage is a singing contest among human pets that's organized by aliens who have taken over the world and use humans as a source of entertaiment.


how does it work?

there are multiple rounds where humans face off against each other 1 vs 1. the winner proceeds onto the next round while the loser is eliminated. aliens in the crowd vote for the contestant they wish to win, the votes determine the winner, the more one tries the please the crowd of aliens, the better their chances at victory and survival are
