Spanish cuisine is a vibrant mix of flavors and textures, heavily influenced by Mediterranean ingredients such as olive oil, seafood, and cured meats. Tapas culture encourages sharing small plates like patatas bravas, jamón ibérico, and gambas al ajillo, while regional dishes like paella from Valencia and gazpacho from Andalusia highlight local traditions. Spain is also famous for its wine and sherry, as well as sweet treats like churros dipped in thick hot chocolate.
