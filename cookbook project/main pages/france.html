French cuisine is renowned for its elegance, refined techniques, and rich flavors, with an emphasis on high-quality ingredients and careful preparation. From buttery croissants and coq au vin to delicate soufflés and ratatouille, French food ranges from rustic countryside dishes to sophisticated haute cuisine. Cheese and wine play a central role, with regions like Bordeaux and Burgundy producing some of the world’s finest varieties.
