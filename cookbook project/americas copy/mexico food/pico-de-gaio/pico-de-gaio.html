Pico de Gallo, also known as "salsa fresca," is a traditional Mexican salsa made with fresh ingredients. Unlike blended salsas, Pico de Gallo is chunky, combining diced tomatoes, onions, cilantro, lime juice, and chili peppers. It’s a versatile topping for tacos, burritos, grilled meats, or simply enjoyed with tortilla chips.

How to Make Pico de Gallo

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes (diced)
  • 1/2 red or white onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • 1 jalapeño or serrano pepper (finely diced, seeds removed for less heat)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced, optional)
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Ingredients:

Dice the tomatoes, onion, and jalapeño. Chop the cilantro and mince the garlic if using.

2. Mix the Ingredients:

In a bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, onion, jalapeño, and cilantro. Add minced garlic if desired.

3. Season the Salsa:

Stir in lime juice, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to combine.

4. Let It Rest:

Allow the salsa to sit for 10-15 minutes to enhance the flavors.

5. Serve and Enjoy:

Serve fresh with tacos, burritos, grilled dishes, or tortilla chips!
