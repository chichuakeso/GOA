Sopa de Lentejas (Lentil Soup) is a traditional and comforting dish in many Latin American countries, especially in Mexico. It’s made with lentils, vegetables, and aromatic spices, creating a rich, flavorful, and nutritious soup. The versatility of lentils makes this soup both filling and hearty, while being quick and easy to prepare.

How to Make Sopa de Lentejas

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried lentils (rinsed)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 carrots (diced)
  • 1 potato (peeled and diced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (optional)

Instructions:

1. Sauté the Vegetables:

Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, cooking until softened (about 3-4 minutes).

2. Add the Vegetables and Spices:

Add the diced carrots, potato, tomato, cumin, paprika, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.

3. Cook the Lentils:

Add the rinsed lentils and vegetable broth to the pot. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 25-30 minutes, or until the lentils and vegetables are tender.

4. Adjust the Seasoning:

Remove the bay leaf, then taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.

5. Serve and Enjoy:

Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro if desired. Enjoy your warm and comforting Sopa de Lentejas!
