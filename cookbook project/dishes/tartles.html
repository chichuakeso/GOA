These chocolate hazelnut tartlets are made with only 7]a few simple ingredients, many of which you likely have in your pantry. The crust consists of ground raw hazelnuts, a bit of flour, and some melted butter. I used vegan butter, but you can use any butter. Want to try making your own at home? Follow this simple step-by-step recipe:

How to Make Tartles

Ingredients:

  • Raw hazelnuts
  • Coconut milk
  • Dark chocolate
  • Butter

Instructions:

1. Add the ground hazelnuts:

flour, sugar, salt, and melted vegan butter to a bowl and mix to combine

2. Split the dough in half:

Make sure the dough is evenly distributed, and there are no holes that you can see.

3. open up a can of coconut milk.:

Scoop out the thick coconut cream from the top. Measure out half a cup and transfer to a pan.

4. whisk to combine.:

The liquid should be thick and creamy. Add the vanilla and whisk to combine.

5. Add a pinch of crushed hazelnuts:

Place in the fridge to set for 2 hours.
