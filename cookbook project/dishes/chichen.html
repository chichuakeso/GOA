Spanish chicken recipe is a beautiful marriage of Mediterranean flavors, where tender chicken meets the bold characters of Spanish paprika, garlic, and olive oil. Want to try making your own at home? Follow this simple step-by-step recipe:

How to Make Spanish Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 150g chorizo
  • 1 red pepper
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 150g pitted green olives
  • 400g can butter beans

Instructions:

1. Heat the oven to 200C/180C fan/gas 6:

Heat the olive oil in a large ovenproof frying pan over a medium heat and fry the onion, chorizo and peppers along with a pinch of salt and pepper for 15 mins until the veg has softened and the chorizo has released its oils. Add the paprika and garlic, and cook for another few minutes until fragrant.

2. Tip in the chopped tomatoes, olives and butter beans, stir to combine and season.:

Nestle in the chicken thighs and season well. Transfer to the oven and bake for 40 mins until the chicken skin is crisp and the meat cooked through and tender. Scatter with the parsley and serve.

3. enjoy!
