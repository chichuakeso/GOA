Salsa verde is a classic herby Italian condiment from the Piedmont region that’s often paired with grilled meats or veggies. While there seem to be as many versions as there are cooks, anchovies, hard boiled eggs, and other non-vegan ingredients can sometimes be a part of the sauce. Want to try making your own at home? Follow this simple step-by-step recipe:

How to Make Grilled Seitan

Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced in half
  • 1 Tbsp red wine vinegar2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tsp capers
  • ½ tsp red miso
  • 1 cup flat leaf parsley, packed

Instructions:

1. Toast the Bread:

Toast the slices of bread until golden and crispy.

2. Mash the Avocado:

In a bowl, mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, and black pepper.

3. Assemble the Toast:

Spread the mashed avocado evenly on the toasted bread.

4. Add Toppings (Optional):

Top with cherry tomatoes, radishes, seeds, or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for extra flavor.

5. Serve and Enjoy:

Enjoy immediately as a healthy breakfast or snack.
