How to Make Ratatouille

Ingredients:

  • Red Onion
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Eggplant
  • Zucchini
  • Red Bell Peppers
  • Fresh Thyme
  • Lemon

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Sauce:

In a saucepan, heat some olive oil over medium heat

2. Simmer the Tomato Base:

Pour in the tomato passata or tomato sauce. Season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste.

3. Slice Your Vegetables:

Aim for slices about a fifth of an inch thick, or thinner if preferred.

4. Assemble the Ratatouille:

Arrange the vegetable slices in the casserole dish in an overlapping circular pattern

5. Bake the Ratatouille:

Place the casserole dish in the preheated oven and bake for 30-40 minutes.
