Spanish Rice and Beans uses pantry staples to create a nutritious, flavorful one pot meal. Vegetarian, gluten free, and so simple to make, this rice and beans recipe will become a staple. Want to try making your own at home? Follow this simple step-by-step recipe:

How to Make Rice and Beans

Ingredients:

  • White rice
  • Beans
  • Aromatics
  • canned tomatoes
  • Spices
  • Olives
  • Lemon Juice

Instructions:

1. Cook the Aromatics:

Start by heating some olive oil in a large skillet with a fitted top over medium heat.

2. Bloom the Spices:

Cooking spices in hot oil helps unlock their full flavor potential.

3. Toast the Rice

Stir in the rice and let it cook for about 2 minutes, which helps deepen its flavor.

4. Add the Tomatoes, Beans, and Broth:

Simmer the mixture for about 25 minutes, or until the liquid is absorbed and rice is tender
