Mona Lisa

main info

The woman behind the Mona Lisa's subject is Lisa Gherardini, the wife of Florentine merchant Francesco del Giocondo. Lisa was born on June 15, 1479, in Florence, Italy, into a wealthy family. She married Francesco at the age of 16, and they had five children together.The painting was believed to have been painted between 1503 and 1506; however, Leonardo may have continued working on it as late as 1517. King Francis I of France acquired the Mona Lisa after Leonardo's death in 1519, and it is now the property of the French Republic.
