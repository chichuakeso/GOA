What is Hackathon?

hackathon is an event where people
engage in rapid and collaborative
engineering over a relatively short period
of time such as 24 or 48 hours.
The goal of a hackathon is to create
functioning software or hardware by the
end of the event.there is no restriction on
the type of software being created or the
design of the new system.
In addition to creating functional software
or hardware,hackathons can help
participants develop skills like problem
solving,critical thinking,creativity, team
work,communication and time management.
